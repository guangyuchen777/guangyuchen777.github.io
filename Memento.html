Memento Mori

Technology & Realization

Business & Entreprenership

User & Society

Project: Private

Group: Anouk Greven & Guangyu Chen

Although we all will eventually be confronted with death, be it of our parents, family or friends, death is still a taboo. Most people don’t want to talk about it, because they see it as a sad topic they want to stay far away from. However, avoiding discussing death will leave us vulnerable and unprepared when the moment arrives. That’s why we developed a board game, Memento Mori, to help people to talk about death in a way which is natural, without the sadness and heaviness that is often associated with dying and even with a twist of humor.
