Humidity

In this research the human ability to assess domestic humidity is investigated together with assessment of the factors and actions within the home that can influence this humidity. It is combined with explorations examining the effectiveness of feedback prototypes in the human ability to address the humidity and create a safe environment. It presents an opportunity to design for humidity awareness within domestic environments, as humidity affects peopleâ€™s health on different aspects ranging from physical health (virus spread) and mental health (mood). The research builds upon the data-enabled design approach, resulting in the deployment of a peripheral humidity indicator prototype. It concludes with a design direction that aims to generate awareness and thereby aid people in creating an environment that is beneficial for health in terms of humidity levels.
