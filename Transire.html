Transire

The COVID pandemic has caused immense changes in our lifestyle, and has taken a toll on many lives. In our current society it feels more important than ever to stay connected with each other, even when you’re not physically with someone. The design, Transire, allows users to interact with a tangible device in certain ways, replicating the presence of someone who’s passed, in order to maintain connection with that someone, easing them through the grieving process.
