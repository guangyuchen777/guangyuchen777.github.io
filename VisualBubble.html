Misinformation includes all false or inaccurate information that is spread on social media. In recent years, Artificial Intelligence (AI) has become an efficient tool for creating misinformation on social media. It is logical to assume that bots spreading would be wider than human spreading. However, false news spreading quicker, deeper and broader than truth is mainly due to humans, not AI bots. In this research, the social media users’ behaviors are analyzed by means of a user journey. Next, a design probe is created from the perspective of reflection-oriented user experience. This design probe was tested with users to determine its effectiveness in increasing users’ awareness of being exposed to misinformation. The initial findings are that this design probe can contribute to more critical attitudes towards the news that users are exposed to and, therefore, have the potential to mitigate social media misinformation.
